## What Nutrication is

Nutrication scores food on a 0-100 scale and assigns an A-D grade using four
published frameworks (NOVA processing, Nutri-Score, glycemic load, and
ingredient flags). It also offers meal ideas, swap suggestions, restaurant
menu analysis, and a day-by-day plate tracker.

## What Nutrication is not

**Nutrication is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.** The grades,
warnings, and meal suggestions are general nutrition information. They
should not replace guidance from your doctor, dietitian, or any licensed
healthcare professional. If you have a medical condition, allergy, or
dietary restriction, follow your care team's advice — not the app's.

We do our best to estimate macros (especially for restaurant menus and
meal photos), but these are AI-derived estimates and can be wrong by
30% or more. Cross-check with package labels and your own measurement
when accuracy matters.
