# Nutrication

**Honest food grades, backed by science.**

Nutrication scores every food A–D using four independent frameworks: NOVA
processing, Nutri-Score, glycemic load, and ingredient flags. Scan a barcode,
photograph a label, snap a meal, or upload a restaurant menu — get an honest
grade in seconds.

## Built on

- **NOVA** — Monteiro et al., processing-level classification
- **Nutri-Score** — Hercberg et al., aggregate nutritional quality
- **Glycemic Load** — Jenkins et al., blood-sugar impact
- **Ingredient flags** — IARC monographs, EFSA panels, EWG databases
